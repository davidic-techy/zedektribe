The Mandate

A People of the Presence

The Priestly Core is the engine room of the Tribe. This is where we learn to handle the Word of God with accuracy and sustain the fire of prayer with stamina.

We do not believe in casual Christianity. We are building a people who are spiritually fit, doctrinally sound, and burning with a love that cannot be quenched.

"No one survives the wilderness alone."

In this expression, we solve the crisis of spiritual loneliness. You will not just attend meetings; you will be known, you will be covered, and you will walk with brothers and sisters who sharpen your iron.
